Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change player names?

Go to Settings > Change Player Names to update your names at any time.

What's included in the Premium version?

The Premium version includes access to all games, unlimited play, and future content updates.

How do I restore my Premium purchase?

Go to Settings > Premium and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore your Premium access.
